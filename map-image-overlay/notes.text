Start Page: A welcoming screen with an introduction to Pittsburgh's history and the importance of the landmarks featured on the map.
Map with Markers: Users will see the city of Pittsburgh displayed on the map with markers for historical sites, parks, museums, and rivers.
Click on Markers: When a user clicks on a marker, they’ll see:
A description of the place.
A timeline of events.
Fun facts or historical trivia.
Optional: Audio clips or images for a more interactive experience.
Routing Feature: Allow users to create their own routes for walking tours or suggested routes highlighting certain historical periods or themes (e.g., Pittsburgh’s industrial revolution route).
